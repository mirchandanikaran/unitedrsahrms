Employee Management System - How To Use

User-focused guide for employees, managers, HR, admin, and leadership.

HTML Guide

1) Getting started

  1. Open the portal URL (local dev: http://localhost:3000).
  2. Sign in with your work email and password.
  3. Use logout in the top bar when finished.

Dates are displayed as DD-MMM-YY across the UI.

2) Roles at a glance

RoleTypical access
EmployeeLeaves, profile requests, onboarding, reporting line, awards/social participation.
ManagerEmployee access + team leave approvals.
HREmployees, attendance, leaves, reports, onboarding, profile reviews, analytics, moderation.
AdminFull control including holiday event manager, department manager, and organization reporting.
LeadershipExecutive dashboard/analytics, org-wide reporting, awards/social participation.

3) Leave calendar

  • Active (blue): Monday to Saturday working days.
  • Inactive (grey): Sundays, holidays, and approved leave days.
  • Use month navigation arrows to move between months.
  • Check Upcoming inactive days for planning.
Pending leave requests do not appear until approved.

4) Leaves

Employees

  • Apply for leave with type, dates, and reason.
  • Track pending, approved, and rejected status.

Managers / HR / Admin

  • Approve or reject requests.
  • Add a clear rejection reason when rejecting.

5) Employees and departments (Admin/HR)

Employees

  • Add, edit, search, export, and soft-remove employees.

Department manager (Admin)

  • Open Employees → Manage Departments.
  • Add department types (name, code, description).
  • Edit existing department types as needed.

6) Awards

  • Admin/HR: grant and remove award badges.
  • All users: view badges and recognition history.

7) Social Wall

  • Create short posts.
  • Like and comment on posts.
  • Delete your own posts/comments.
  • Admin/HR can moderate by deleting inappropriate content.

8) Other key modules

Onboarding

Employees complete checklist items; admin/HR can initialize onboarding for new hires.

My Profile

Employees submit profile change requests; HR/Admin approve or reject.

Reporting

View manager chain and direct reports; admin/leadership can view org-wide structure.

Analytics & Reports

Leadership metrics and downloadable CSVs for HR/Admin.

9) Quick tips

  • Apply leave early and check inactive day highlights before planning.
  • Use Awards and Social Wall for positive engagement.
  • Log out on shared machines.
